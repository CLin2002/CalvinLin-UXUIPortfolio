Film Showcase

Entertainment

Synopsis


Entertainment by Jeremy Chu is a nostalgic and artful tribute to the iconic Vancouver nightclub, Marco Polo. Marco Polo defined Vacnouver’s Chinatown nightlife in the 1960s and 1970s. Taking inspiration from Jeremy Chu, a Vancouver poet, we hope to highlight the impact of the legendary establishment and its impact on Vancouver’s communities of colour. Through his words and stunning visuals, the film strives to resurrect the soul of the famous nightclub and the unforgettable atmosphere that surrounded it. Despite closing their doors in 1982, Marco Polo has made its mark in Vancouver Chinatown’s history, as a place for music, dance, comedy, and cuisine. This film celebrates the vibrant spirit of Marco Polo and the lasting impacts it has on the city’s history.


Personal contributions


I contributed to this project by setting up lighting and set design. During post production I worked on the title and end credit screens, as well as subtitles and video and audio editing.
