Objective


Develop a comprehensive overview of project funding and timelines. Design and produce visually engaging and easily comprehensible infographics for both public and internal audiences. Ensure infographics effectively communicate key project milestones, funding sources, and financial allocations, and are completed within a specified timeframe for each phase.


Understanding The Problem


Reading lengthy, dense factsheets can be tedious and time-consuming, especially for farmers and producers who often have little time to spare. This challenge is even more pronounced during the busy summer season when production takes precedence. How can we create straightforward, yet impactful infographics to better engage farmers and producers with our content?

Gathering Insights


Our first task was to gain insights into how users currently planned their travels. We found that most users felt unsafe on public transit, especially at night. Users of Google and Apple maps noted that ETAs are unreliable and often inaccurate, emphasizing that planning a trip with multiple participants can be daunting and time-consuming.

Framing The Approach


Drawing from the insights we gathered, we devised plans on developing a new feature that Citymapper could implement to address the issues that users faced when using public transit. It became evident that even experienced transit riders could not optimize their schedule as ETAs are often unreliable.

This informed our framing of “How might we streamline group trips by removing the time-consuming planning phase?”


Introducing Citymapper Group ETA


Embedded within Citymapper’s existing interface, Group ETA is a feature that plans the most optimal route for users within the same group. By providing a unique route for different members of a group, it minimizes potential wait times, allowing users to focus on the day ahead of them, rather than on waiting.

Throughout the implementation of this feature, I was responsible for designing the concept, interface design, as well as contribute towards our usability tests.

Features of Group ETA

Inviting and Joining Group


Inviting and joining a group retains the original Citymapper function to share links using messaging apps. This helps maintain consistent behavioural patterns and establishes a connection to the existing interace.


Stress-free Planning


Planning a large trip with many participants is as easy as choosing your group and starting the trip. Members who are participating in the group will always have the option to opt out of a trip or hide their status.


Takeaways


Designing for an existing platform is difficult. The most difficult part of this project was to develop a feature that enhanced the existing user experience without compromising the original integrity of the app.


Privacy is important. Protecting user data and being transparent about information sharing should always be a top priority in design.


The feature should be easily accessible. Any valuable feature should be instantly recognizable and easily accessible. Users should not need to navigate through multiple screens to perform a task.
